Welcome to the Inuyasha fan page!

Description

Inuyasha is a Japanese TV series called anime. The TV series started in October of 2000 and ended in September of 2004 with 167 episodes and 6 seasons and the episodes are about 20 minutes long, this was the first act.

There was a second act that started in October of 2009 called Inuyasha: The Final Act which had 26 episodes and ended in March of 2010, this was to finish off the manga series.

Side note: The second act was Directed by: Yasunao Aoko and Produced by: Tomoyuki Saito, Mitomu Asai, and Naohiro Ogata.

Characters

There are a total of 6 main characters who are constantly in the series:

  • Kirara
  • Sango
  • Shippo
  • Kagome
  • Miroku
  • Inuyasha

Kagome is the main female character of the show while, of course, Inuyasha is the main male figure of the show. Fun fact: 'Inu' means dog in Japanese and 'yasha' means deamon.

There are a few characters that are not necissarily main characters but they appear quite often including some villains:

  • Kikyo
  • Sesshomaru
  • Jaken
  • Rin
  • A-Un
  • Kohaku
  • Koga
  • Naraku

Sesshomaru is actually Inuyasha's older brother and comes off as the 'evil' villain that shows up periodically throughout the series trying to capture the famous sword Inuyasha was destined to have from his father.

Summary

Inuyasha is a fun and kid friendly cartoon that children and adults can watch and enjoy. As the show goes, it teaches communication techniques showing children than if we are angry we have choices such as taking our time with how we respond by taking a moment or a day to ourselves or by talking it out right away. The show focuses on accepting one another for who we are and even though we all have our differences we can interact with each other with respect.
